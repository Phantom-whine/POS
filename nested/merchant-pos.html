Who is Eligible for Merchant POS Loan in UAE

Merchant POS Loan is designed for UAE businesses of all sizes that accept card payments. Here's what you need to qualify:

UAE-registered business with valid trade license
Open to all business types: retail, F&B, e-commerce, services
Consistent monthly card sales (USD 50,000+)
Minimum 12 months of trading history
No collateral or personal guarantor required
