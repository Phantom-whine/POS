Credit Pre-
Assessment

Structured. Detailed. Insight-Driven.

Our Credit Pre-assessment gives us a clear understanding of your business's financial standing, credit behavior, and operational stability. This internal evaluation helps us determine which lenders and products match your profile, thereby saving time and increasing efficiency of the application process.

We combine financial analysis, credit parameters, business sector indicators, and verification steps to build a clear picture of your funding readiness.

Why Our Credit
Pre-assessment Matters

Every bank in the UAE has different criteria for SME lending. Submitting your case without understanding where you stand often leads to delays, wasted time or 'rejection' in the worst scenario.

Profile Clarity

Understand how lenders assess your credit strength, income stability, and overall approval.

Improvement Areas

Identify the specific gaps in your credit profile, income, or documents that may affect approval.

Perfect Match

Get matched with lenders that best suit your profile, goals, and borrowing capacity.

Assessment Pillars

Each business is assessed across core pillars that reflect its ability and willingness to repay.

Financial Performance

Very High Influence

We analyze:

  • • Revenue consistency
  • • Cash-flow health
  • • Working capital cycle
  • • Debt-servicing ability

Strong financial performance has the greatest impact on your credit profile.

Ownership & Management Strength

(Medium Influence)

We evaluate:

  • • Years in business
  • • Experience and leadership stability
  • • Owner commitment & financial investment in business

Businesses with strong management show lower long-term risk.

Market & Credit History

High Influence

We look at:

  • • Repayment history with previous loans
  • • Market reputation and supplier relations
  • • Industry sector stability and growth potential

Clean credit history is vital for securing competitive interest rates.

Final Summary Report

After the evaluation, your business receives a clear strategy & plan of action.

You also receive a summary outlining key strengths and fixing any potential red flags. We help you improve your credit standing over time.
