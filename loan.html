What These Loan Terms Mean

Term
Simple Meaning
Principal
The loan amount your business receives.
Tenure
Number of months you'll repay the loan.
Flat Rate
Profit/interest calculated on the full loan amount for the whole term.
Reducing Rate (APR)
Profit/interest calculated only on the remaining balance, which reduces each month.
