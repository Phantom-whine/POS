Senior Relationship Officer – SME Banking & Finance

Full Time · Dubai, UAE
Apply Now
About the Role

We are seeking a highly motivated Senior Relationship Officer to join our dynamic team. In this role, you will be responsible for acquiring new SME clients, understanding their financial needs, and providing tailored banking and financing solutions.

Key Responsibilities
  • Identify and acquire new SME clients across the UAE market.
  • Analyze client financial data to recommend suitable loan and facility products.
  • Manage the end-to-end loan application and approval process.
  • Build and maintain strong, long-lasting client relationships.
  • Achieve monthly and quarterly business development targets.
Requirements
  • Minimum 3-5 years of experience in SME Banking or Corporate Finance in the UAE.
  • Proven track record of achieving sales targets.
  • Strong understanding of financial statements and credit analysis.
  • Excellent communication and negotiation skills.
